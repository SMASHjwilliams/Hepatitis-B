Jared Williams

SMASH@Wharton scholar Jared Williams is a member of the inaugural class of 2021. Jared enjoys learning and practicing new skills as he prepares for his career: “My goal in life is to be a chemical/biomolecular engineer.” Jared believes challenging himself with hard work, keeping a positive mindset, and carefully choosing friends who share similarly demanding life goals are the foundations of success. Jared says that his favorite inspirational quote is: “Nothing gets easier you just get better”.

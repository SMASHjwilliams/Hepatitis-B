How Math Applies To Hepatitis B

Hello. Our names are Jared Williams, Jada-Nicole Witherspoon and Nasir Duppins. Together as a three person team we are working on bettering the community by working on ways to limit the spread of Hepatitis B. We have come up with a solution through empathizing with Philadelphian community, defining the problem, ideating ideas, prototyping based the on needs of our area, and finally testing. We would like you to implement our idea in the philadelphia area.

The impoverished population in Philadelphia has exponentially less than the upper class and henceforth can't afford as much. They rely on what is given to them to survive as the wages to the jobs they have not enough to be self-sufficient.
